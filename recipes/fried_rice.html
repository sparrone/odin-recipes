Fried Rice

Serves 1 person, I think

Ingredients

  • Cooked, day old cold rice
  • Cooking fat(oil, bacon grease, any fat that can withstand high heat)
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Onion(Green, yellow, shallot, whatever you want)
  • Literally any vegetable
  • Literally any meat you want
  • Soy Sauce
  • Any sauce or condiment that you like(Oyster sauce, sriracha, etc)
  • White pepper

Steps

  1. Get a wok or any pan nice and hot. Slice your vegetables and meats thin.
    Beat your egg. Have everything ready to add to the pan, the cooking goes fast
  2. Put cold fat in hot pan. Add aromatics, meat, and vegetables in that general
    order. Everything cooks at a different rate, and everyone has different
    preferences on the doneness of each ingredient. Use your best judgement here.
    I recommend not undercooking your meats, unless you like shitting your brains out.
  3. When these ingredients are close to desired doneness, make well in center of
    pan and add beaten egg, scrambling it. You can also just crack the egg directly
    into the pan and quickly scramble it, but a pre-beaten egg is easier.
  4. When egg is close to desired doneness, add the rice. Break up any clumps of
    rice. This is easier done before its added to the pan, but you do you.
  5. When rice is good and hot, add your sauces and seasonings. Stir and toss
    vigorously until all the moisture is absorbed or cooked off. Your friend rice
    should not be wet.
  6. Its done. Put in bowl, top with sliced green onions or whatever you want.
