Chicken Katsu

Serves 2 people

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • Flour
  • 1 Egg
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Neutral oil(canola, peanut, etc)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook your rice. Theres a hundred different methods to achieve perfect rice. You can figure it out, I believe in you.
  2. Cut the chicken breast in half to create two thin pieces of chicken
  3. Flatten each half of chicken breast. Cover halved chicken breast with plastic wrap,
    butcher paper, or place in ziploc bag. We don't want to spray raw chicken juices
    everywhere. Flatten pieces of chicken with mallet, bottom of skillet, or rolling pin.
  4. Salt both sides of chicken
  5. Coat both sides of chicken with flour
  6. Dip chicken in beaten egg. Make sure all parts of chicken are covered in egg
  7. Coat egg-covered chicken with panko breadcrumbs. Press as many breadcrumbs into
    chicken as possible. We want a thick coating of crumbs.
  8. Fry the chicken in the oil. I'll add more desription here later.

And thats it. You have your rice and your chicken katsu. Top with whatever sauce you want,
maybe add some sliced green onions if you're feeling fancy. Do whatever you want with it.
